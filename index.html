How the journey works

Start simple,
build from there

Each week builds on the last. We start with low-effort, high-reward formats so you get tangible wins fast, then move into the more executional stuff once you've got the feel for it.

Week 1

Consistency & finding your rhythm

Kick off your creator era with formats that are easy to execute but high upside — get comfortable with layout, captions, fonts and the overall vibe of a video.

Focus: memey hook-to-demo + long hooks
Week 2

Finding your voice & building credibility

Start practising talking-head content — back what you say with proof, work on your delivery, your style and showing up as someone worth following.

Focus: talking-head angles + quality feedback
